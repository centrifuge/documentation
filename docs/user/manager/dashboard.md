# Dashboard overview

The Dashboard provides a consolidated view of your share class operations, giving you quick visibility into NAV, pending orders, and holdings.

## What the dashboard shows

- **NAV section** - Current total NAV, price per share, and performance chart
- **Orders summary** - Pending and approved investments and redemptions
- **Holdings preview** - Asset holdings across all networks

![NAV Section](/assets/images/top_dashboard.png)

## Daily monitoring workflow

1. **Check NAV** - Verify current NAV and price are accurate
2. **Review pending investments** - See if new deposits need approval
3. **Review pending redemptions** - Check for redemption requests
4. **Verify approved orders** - Confirm approved orders are ready for processing
5. **Check holdings** - Ensure adequate liquidity for operations

## Orders summary

![Orders summary cards](/assets/images/order_overview_dashboard.png)

| Metric | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Pending investments** | Total value of deposits awaiting approval |
| **Approved investments** | Approved deposits awaiting share issuance |
| **Pending redemptions** | Redemption requests awaiting approval |
| **Approved redemptions** | Estimated payout for approved redemptions |

## Holdings preview

![Holdings table preview](/assets/images/holding_table_dashboard.png)

The holdings section shows asset holdings across all networks with quantity and value for each holding. This helps you quickly assess liquidity.

## Quick actions

From the dashboard, you can:
- **Update NAV** - Click the "Update NAV" button to open the NAV modal
- **Navigate to details** - Click on any section to go to the full page

## Understanding the metrics

**Pending Investments** - Sum of all pending deposits across vaults, converted to pool currency. Your next action: Approve investments.

**Approved Investments** - Deposits approved but shares not yet issued. Your next action: Issue shares.

**Pending Redemptions** - Shares waiting for approval to redeem. Your next action: Approve redemptions.

**Approved Redemptions** - Approved redemptions awaiting payout. Your next action: Revoke shares and process payout.
